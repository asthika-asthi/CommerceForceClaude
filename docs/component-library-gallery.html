PLATE V

Tri Star Originals

11 specimens

The coarse-wrapped pieces of the live Tri Star homepage — copy and layout are fixed, not props-driven. Text/accent colours use real theme tokens, but several backgrounds are intentionally hardcoded white, frozen for pixel-identity and untouched by the dark-theme sweep — not a template to hand a dark-branded client. The brand switch above still moves their accent colours; it won't move those fixed white panels. Four (marked ) receive the homepage's real product/category data automatically.
